About Us

Established in 2010, Monash Curry n Tandoor is an Indian restaurant specialising in North Indian Food.
We are situated next to the Monash Aquatic centre in Glen Waverley with ample car parking space nearby.
Our aim is to provide quality food and great service that makes your dining experience a memorable one.
Simply… City of Monash meets great Indian food, and so Monash Curry n Tandoor was born.
